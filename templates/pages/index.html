Who are we?

A system that allows users review companies based on their employment and interview experiences. Users should be able to comment about their work experiences, share pros and cons of the company, their position and salary information, interview process etc. These posts may be anonymous if sharing user once it to be. users may follow companies they are interested in. Companies should be able to do post job offerings, and submit requests to remove reviews about it. Admins will decide whether that post should be removed.
